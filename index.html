David's life

As we all know David Jason is an english actor best know for only fools and horses he has also stared in many more shows like open all hours and a touch of frost.

Early life

Not many people know this but David had a twin brother who died during child birth . David has an older brother Arthur White and wanted to follow in his footsteps and become an actor but his father suggested he should get a trade first so for 6years he trained as an electrician.

Honours

In 1993, David was made an Officer of the Order of the British Empire(OBE) he was also knighted for services for acting and comedy
